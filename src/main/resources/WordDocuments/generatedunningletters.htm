Generate Dunning Letters

 >  >  >  >

Generate Dunning Letters allow the AR Collector to generate progressively stronger letters to be sent to customers asking for payment of past due invoices. A Dunning Campaign is associated with an Award and a Dunning Letter Template is associated with a Dunning Campaign.

    Only users with the AR Collector role can create Dunning Letters.
