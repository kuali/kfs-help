Business Rules

•      Invoices will be created when the Award is within it’s defined Billing Frequency and has not been previously billed for that period.

•      When Invoicing Option is Invoice by Award and there is more than one account, all accounts must have the same Contract Control Account.

•      When Invoicing Option is Invoice by Contract Control Account, each account that will be invoiced must have a Contract Control Account.

•      Award must be active.

•      One or more accounts associated with the Award must be active.

•      Invoices will not be created when Final Bill is Yes.

•      If Billing Frequency is Milestone, one or more milestones must have a Completed Date in the current billing period.

•      If Billing Frequency is Pre-Determined Billing Schedule, one more bills must have a Bill Date in the current billing period.

•      Agency must be associated with a valid AR Customer.

•      One or more acounts on the awards must have an amount to bill, either postive or negative.

•      Organization associated with the Award must have Organization Options and Organization Accounting Defaults set up.

•      Invoices will not be created if the Award has invoices already in process: Initiated, Saved, Enroute or Exception.

•      The following will cause Contracts & Grants invoices to suspend:

o  Bill Date exceeds the Award Stop Date.

o  New Total Billed Amount exceeds Award Total Amount.

o  Invoice Amount is less than Invoice Minimum.

o  Agency Primary Address is missing or incomplete.

o  Agency Alternate Address is incomplete.

o  Object Codes used on the account are not assigned to Cost Categories.

o  Letter of Credit Remaining Amount is not sufficient (move to LOC Review?)

o  Award has expired account with expenditures in the billing period.

o  Award has closed account with current expenditures in the billing period.

o  Award has Stop Work set to Yes.

o  Reports or supporting documentation are required to be attached and are not.

•      When Invoicing Option is set to Invoice by Award one invoice will be created for the award and General Ledger Pending Entries will be created for each account assigned to the award.

•      When Invoicing Option is set to Invoice by Account one invoice will be created for each account assigned to the award and a General Ledger Pending Entries created for each account.

•      When Invoicing Option is set to Invoice by Contract Control Account an invoice will be created for each Contract Control Account associated with the accounts on the Award. General Ledger Pending Entries will be created for each Contract Control Account.
